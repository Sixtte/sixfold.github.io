Preparations.

Hopefully at the beginning of September I am planning to move house. When I hopefully get the go-ahead on Wednesday I will be giving in my notice to my currently letting agent and preparing to move out. This will mean lots and lots of packing.

With moving I will have to make a lot of preparations towards getting a van to move all the big bits as well as moving all the tiny bits in my car. To Aid with all this stress my current letting agent refuses to give a reference until we have provided our notice of leaving the property, which makes it fairly difficult for us as we don't wish to be without house...

After we have got the house we now then to move all the bills and so on over to take affect for this move in date that we have been given and to hopefully allow for a smooth transition. This may be a little less easy than it seems as I know need to move all my bills over to the new address and make sure that we have everything we need like electricity and water etc...

Hopefully by the end of September I will be moved in and be on-top of everything to the extend that all is well and I have everything back in check. After we move in hopefully we will be much more comfortable and relaxed and properly make the place a home.
